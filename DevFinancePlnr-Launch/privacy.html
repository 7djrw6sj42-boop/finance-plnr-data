1. Who We Are

This privacy policy describes how Donnie Gillespie (“we,” “us,” “our”) handles information when you use the Developer Finance Plnr mobile application (the “App”).

Contact:
DeveloperFinancePlnr@Atomicmail.io

The Short Version
  • We don't have a server. We don't collect your data.
  • Everything you enter (deals, budgets, contracts, addresses, your API keys, your saved records) stays on your device only.
  • The App makes calls to public third-party APIs (Census, BLS, FBI, FRED, Yahoo Finance, Zippopotam.us, NETROnline, Anthropic, RentCast) on your behalf. Those services have their own privacy policies.
  • We do not use ads, trackers, or analytics.

2. What We Don't Do

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior in the App.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data — because we don't collect any.
  • We do not include third-party advertising SDKs or tracking pixels.
  • We do not access your photos, microphone, or contacts without explicit permission for a specific feature (camera/library access only when you choose to attach a photo to a Driving for Dollars lead).

3. Information You Provide That Stays On Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your phone using the operating system's secure storage:

  • Deal inputs — purchase price, ARV, rehab cost, rental income, etc.
  • Rehab budget line items — costs for demo, kitchen, baths, etc.
  • Saved deals & budgets — when you tap "Save"
  • Cash buyers list — names, phones, emails, criteria you enter
  • Driving for Dollars leads — addresses, photos you take, notes
  • Contract field entries — seller info, buyer info, property addresses
  • Your Anthropic API key — if you set up the optional Rehab Helper bot
  • Your RentCast API key — if you set up the optional Comp Pull feature
  • Chat history with the Helper bot — capped at 50 recent messages
  • Cached state-level data — for 7 days, for offline use

All of the above:

  • Lives only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to us (we have no servers to receive it)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App's content (real estate investing, contracts) is intended for adult users.

6. California (CCPA / CPRA) Notice

California residents have specific rights regarding personal information. Because we do not collect personal information, we do not sell or share personal information, and there is no personal information to access or delete on our servers (we have none). All data remains on your device. To delete data on your device, uninstall the App or use the "Wipe All App Data" option in Settings.

7. EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

We process no personal data within the meaning of GDPR. Local data on your device is fully under your control. The third-party APIs listed above are accessed by your device; their use is governed by each provider's own GDPR posture.

8. Data Security

Local data is protected by your device's operating system security (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore equivalents). We strongly recommend:

  • Use a passcode/biometric on your phone
  • Keep iOS or Android updated
  • Don't jailbreak/root your device

We cannot recover data lost from device failure, theft, or uninstall. There is no cloud backup of your saved deals or contracts unless and until cloud sync is added in a future version (you will be notified before such a feature is enabled).

9. App Permissions

The App requests the following permissions only when needed for specific features:

  • Internet access — for the third-party APIs above
  • Camera — only when you choose to take a photo for a Driving for Dollars lead
  • Photo Library — only when you choose to select an existing photo for a lead

If a future version requires additional permissions, you will be asked at the time of the feature request and can decline.

10. In-App Purchases

This version of the App is free. If we add paid features in the future:

  • Purchases will be handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store in-app purchase systems
  • We do not receive your payment card details — those are handled by Apple/Google
  • We may receive a confirmation token from Apple/Google indicating you have an active subscription
  • The corresponding privacy policies of Apple/Google govern the payment process

11. Affiliate Links

The "Investor Tools & Partners" section of the App contains affiliate links to third-party services (lenders, LLC formation, lead generation tools, insurance, CPAs). We may earn a commission if you sign up through these links. This does not affect your price. We only list services we'd recommend regardless of commission.

When you tap a partner link, you leave the App and visit the third party's website. Tracking on those sites (cookies, IDs, etc.) is governed by the third party's privacy policy, not ours. The App does not transmit any data about you to those sites — only the click itself, which is standard outbound web traffic.
