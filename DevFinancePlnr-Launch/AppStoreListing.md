## Description (4000 char max)

> **The complete toolkit for real estate wholesalers and BRRRR investors.**
>
> Stop juggling 6 different apps and a Google Sheet. Developer Finance Plnr puts deal analysis, market data, contracts, and rehab budgeting in one place — designed by investors, for investors.
>
> **What's inside:**
>
> 🏠 **Deal Analyzer (BRRRR)**
> Run the numbers on any deal in 30 seconds. Calculate cash-on-cash return, cap rate, monthly cash flow, equity gained, cash left in deal, and refinance projections. Built around the 70% rule.
>
> 🔨 **Rehab Budget Tool**
> 10 detailed line-item categories — Demo, Exterior, Systems, Kitchen, Baths, Flooring, Walls/Paint, Doors, Lighting, Permits. Auto-compares your budget to your Deal Analyzer projection and flags variance over 15%. Cost-per-sqft + contingency built in.
>
> 📊 **Market Scorecard with Live Data**
> Type a city + state. We pull real-time data from US Census, Bureau of Labor Statistics, and FBI to score the market: population growth, unemployment, property tax, income tax, violent crime, education rank, and landlord-friendliness. Auto-rates 7 metrics + lets you rate 3 local conditions.
>
> 📍 **Property Lookup**
> Enter any US address. ZIP code auto-fills city + state. One tap to open the property in Zillow, Redfin, or county records. Compare value estimates side by side.
>
> 📝 **Wholesale Contract Generator**
> Generate a Purchase & Sale Agreement with assignment clause. 23 standard sections. Attorney-grade language including liquidated damages, specific performance, lis pendens, ESIGN-compliant signature blocks. Share via Messages, email, or PDF.
>
> 🤝 **Assignment Contract Generator**
> Assign your equitable interest to an end buyer. Full assignor/assignee disclosure language for state law compliance.
>
> 🤖 **Rehab Helper Bot** (optional, Claude-powered)
> Ask anything about wholesale deals, BRRRR strategy, permits, contracts, or contractor management. Brings expert guidance to every deal.
>
> 📚 **Reference Library**
> - Wholesaler Master Guide — 10-step legal workflow
> - BRRRR Strategy Guide
> - Scope of Work Template
> - Contractor & Sub Playbook (vetting, payment schedules, red flags, costly mistakes)
> - Seller Scripts (cold call openers, objection handling)
> - Rehab Terminology Glossary (40+ construction terms)
> - Real Estate & Wholesale Glossary (70+ deal terms)
>
> 💰 **Live Markets**
> Daily updates on housing-related stocks (ITB, XHB, VNQ, DHI, LEN, PHM, HD, LOW, BLDR) and interest rates (Fed Funds, 10-yr Treasury, 30-yr & 15-yr mortgage, 30-yr Treasury) — pulled live from FRED and Yahoo Finance.
>
> **💾 Save & Export**
> Save unlimited deals and rehab budgets to your phone. Export any deal, budget, or contract as a PDF — share via email, AirDrop, or print.
>
> **Built for these workflows:**
> - Wholesalers locking up properties under PSA + assigning to cash buyers
> - BRRRR investors recycling capital across rentals
> - Flippers scoping rehabs and managing GCs
> - New investors learning the language and process
>
> **What it isn't:**
> Not legal, tax, or financial advice. Templates are educational starting points — always have a licensed real estate attorney in your state review before signing real contracts.
>
> **Free for personal use.** Optional in-app upgrade for cloud sync, AI helper, and unlimited contract generation (coming soon).
